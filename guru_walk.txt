Marketplace Revenue System —
Implementation Document (Supabase)
This document defines the final revenue and billing logic for the free‑tour marketplace, incorporating
updated credit rules, guide plans, booking deductions, and refund mechanics.
Roles:
• Tourist (Free) — books tours, pays nothing to the platform.
• Guide (Platform Free) — limited capacity & inventory.
• Guide (Pro) — pays platform fees via credits + can boost tours.
Tips are always off‑platform.
Chargebacks are not part of the system.

1. Executive Summary
The platform monetizes Pro Guides through a prepaid credit system.
Key rules:
• 1 Credit = €1
• Platform fee = 2 credits per adult (10+ years old)
• Children under 10 = no platform fee
• Credits deducted at booking time
• Credits refunded if booking cancels within allowed window

5.2 Deduction Function Logic

5.3 Refund Function
